A project materialized in the field

Today, we operate in versatile environments where the constraints are numerous and the technological possibilities infinite. Taking these two elements into account gives us the power to act now and gain an advantage in our markets.

BeOpen IT has been leading companies to conquer this advantage by moving from information to intelligence, from knowledge to decision since 2018 by bringing together the best DevOps, Data and cyber security engineers on the market. Confronted with the reality of the field, we pointed out a huge shortfall in the integration of all teams on a Cloud project directly weakening the two fundamental concepts of cloud computing: continuous integration and continuous deployment.

Our engineers have gathered around this issue with the ambition to overcome all these shortcomings by conceptualizing a unique tool of Onboarding and deployment allowing you to accompany your units to be fully actors of a sustainable success story, profitable, and especially scalable !
